<color=#5E5E56><b>Old Estonians:</b></color> Forgiveness, Forest Spirit, for the hand’s stray; Take this offering, grant the wild its way.
<color=#5E5E56><b>Old Estonians:</b></color> Let shadows hush, let the storms keep; The secrets of roots, where the old ones sleep.
<color=#5E5E56><b>Old Estonians:</b></color> Uphold the farms and the grazing fields; Return the herd health and the pasture yield.
<color=#5E5E56><b>Old Estonians:</b></color> Water the grain, let famine lift; Whisper life to the hearth, and shut the rift.
